Partnering to Deliver Circular Economy Infrastructure for Regional Victoria

Advanced Energy Tech (AET) has developed a game-changing initiative designed to reduce landfill reliance, lower waste management costs, and drive circular economy benefits.

The Challenge

  • • Bendigo landfill is at capacity.
  • • Council & Victorian Government committed to NO new landfills and 80%+ diversion targets.
  • • The region currently lacks appropriate recycling infrastructure to meet these goals.
